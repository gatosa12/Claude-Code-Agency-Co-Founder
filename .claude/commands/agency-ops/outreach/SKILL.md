## Outreach

**Status:** Coming in Phase 2

This skill will help track and manage outreach campaigns by reading:
- `context/agency.md` for agency context
- `context/clients/{client}.md` for client details

### When Ready, This Skill Will:
1. Track warm and cold outreach across channels (LinkedIn, email, phone)
2. Provide message templates adapted to your niche
3. Surface contacts needing follow-up
4. Learn from your feedback to improve future output

Run `/agency-ops:onboarding` first to set up your agency context.
